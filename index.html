Introduction: The Clinical Burden

The weights of unstructured data.

Clinical expertise is often trapped in text. Up to 80% of valuable patient data is stored in unstructured formats—discharge letters, surgical reports, and clinical notes.

What this means: For clinicians and researchers, extracting this data manually is an overwhelming burden that requires years of tedious work. This fragmentation results in "data silos" where critical information remains inaccessible, slowing down life-saving research and limiting the potential for large-scale clinical insights.

The Solution: Bridging the Gap between Data and Science

Intelligent systems that live where your data resides.

We deploy a custom-built intelligence layer directly within your hospital’s secure infrastructure. This system acts as a resident engine that transforms raw, unstructured clinical records into high-fidelity research registries—ensuring that clinical knowledge is unlocked while your data never leaves your control.

By combining medical expertise with auditable AI, we create a transparent pipeline where every extracted value is anchored in clinical evidence and every decision is verifiable by clinical staff.

Case Study: The NEC Registry

Scaling rare disease research with Inselspital Bern.

Necrotising enterocolitis (NEC) is a devastating emergency for premature newborns. Together with the University of Bern, we are building the first automated pipeline to extract over 850 variables from fragmented neonatal records.

  • Status: Active — Pilot phase in progress.
  • Impact: Transforming years of manual abstraction into a scalable, research-ready dataset.
