NEC Registry — Inselspital Bern & University of Bern

Necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC) is a devastating gastrointestinal emergency affecting premature newborns, with significant mortality and long-term morbidity. Despite its severity, structured large-scale registry data on NEC remains scarce — clinical documentation is fragmented across unstructured neonatal records.

In collaboration with the neonatology departments at Inselspital Bern and the University of Bern, MediXtract is building the first automated NEC registry pipeline — extracting structured clinical variables from neonatal records to create a research-ready dataset advancing understanding of NEC risk factors, treatment outcomes, and prevention strategies.

The project follows our full co-development methodology: registry variables are defined jointly with neonatologists using Registry Manager, extraction is performed by MediXtract App, and data quality is governed end-to-end by DLP — with all processing bound to the Zurich region under Swiss nFADP and GDPR.
