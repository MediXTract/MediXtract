Services

What we offer

Five integrated services that transform how your institution designs, populates, audits, and sustains clinical registries — from inception to long-term governance.

Service I
Methodological Support in Registry Design
Impact: Higher-quality registries designed correctly from inception.
Translate clinical research questions into standardised, AI-compatible extraction protocols.
Collaborative development of structured decision trees with clinicians
Clear operational definitions of variables
Minimal physician time commitment through guided validation workflows
Early reduction of ambiguity and downstream data cleaning
Service II
Audit of Historical Clinical Registries
Impact: Increased scientific reliability and improved credibility of past and future publications.
Systematic review of existing databases to detect:
Logical inconsistencies
Misclassification or incomplete documentation
Standardisation gaps across time or departments
Structural weaknesses affecting research validity
Service III
Automated, Reproducible & Traceable Clinical Data Extraction
Impact: Significant reduction in manual abstraction workload and strengthened reproducibility standards.
Deployment of structured AI systems capable of:
We combine In-Context Learning, Chain of Thought reasoning, and multi-step decomposition to extract variables from unstructured records with full auditability. Each variable is extracted 10 times independently — consistency drives expert review prioritisation. Every result is transparent and correctable.
Transparent reasoning pathways per variable
Reproducible outputs supported by consistency metrics
Scalable extraction across large cohorts
Service IV
Continuous Evaluation of Data Collection Systems
Impact: Institutional visibility in data governance innovation and continuous quality improvement.
Development of KPIs to monitor registry integrity and performance.
We maintain and scale your system through iterative 100-variable cycles (80 team hours each): preliminary study, engine tuning, evaluation report, expert feedback. Ongoing maintenance ranges from 5–15 hours/month depending on your SLA.
Periodic quality reports to clinical and research leadership
Identification of recurring ambiguities or systemic weaknesses
Opportunities for methodological publications and conference presentations
Service V
Strengthening Intra- and Inter-Hospital Clinical Collaboration
Impact: Enhanced research networking capacity and stronger academic positioning.
Standardised extraction frameworks enable:
Comparable datasets across departments
Reduced methodological fragmentation
Facilitation of multicenter collaboration and grant competitiveness
Bonus: User-Friendly Web Applications

As an integrated component of the service, we develop dedicated web-based tools that significantly increase usability, reduce physician time burden, and accelerate collaboration.

Intuitive interfaces for clinician validation and feedback
Visualisation of reasoning pathways and data quality metrics
Facilitation of collaborative registry design
Secure, structured cross-departmental interaction
