Solutions

Our suite of tools

1. Architect

The Design & Governance Environment.

The Architect is where the intelligence of the registry is born. It is a specialized, collaborative workspace designed for the architects of clinical research — medical experts and data engineers. Clinicians define each variable's meaning, acceptable values, validation rules, and the decision tree logic used for extraction — through a guided interface that requires no technical knowledge.

Schema Field Editor

Displays field-level validation status. Side-by-side comparison of AI vs. human outputs including Chain of Thought reasoning, consistency metrics, and structured discrepancy classification (Improvements vs. Issues).

Collaborative Decision Tree Design

Clinicians co-design classification pathways with mandatory criteria, supporting criteria, and exclusion rules per variable.

Version Control & Audit Trail

Full history of registry schema changes with rollback and documentation for regulatory review.

2. Extractor

The User-Facing Production Window.

The Extractor is the interface for day-to-day clinical data abstraction. It serves as the high-speed bridge between raw, unstructured documents and validated, research-ready records, surfacing the exact source passage behind every extracted value.

Two-Phase Extraction Pipeline

Processes each record through a two-phase pipeline: first generating a structured case summary, then extracting variables using that summary as enriched context.

Contextual Evidence Display

Every extracted value is anchored to its exact source passage. Full transparency, zero black boxes — verify any value in one click.

Consistency Testing (n=10)

Each variable is run 10 times independently. High consistency = confidence. Low consistency triggers automatic flagging for expert review.

Active Learning Loop

Expert corrections feed back into the extraction logic, compounding accuracy gains with every new case processed.

3. Advisory & Governance (DLP)

Strategic Support & Secure Infrastructure.

Beyond software, MediXtract provides the specialized Advisory and the Data Lifecycle Platform (DLP) required to build and maintain the core intelligence of your registries. We act as your technical partner for the entire data lifecycle.

The Privacy Layer: DLP

The DLP is the privacy and governance layer that sits between raw clinical records and the extraction engine, ensuring every record is de-identified and compliant before AI processing.

Defense-in-Depth Privacy

Scans rendered pixels (OCR) and raw content streams to detect and redact residual PII (names, dates, record IDs) as an automated fail-safe.

Swiss Regional Infrastructure

All processing is bound to the Zurich region (europe-west6), ensuring Swiss nFADP and EU GDPR compliance.

AES-256 Encryption

ISO certified encryption in transit and at rest.

Sustainability

98% Carbon-Free processing in one of the world's cleanest cloud regions.

End-to-End Development

Bespoke design and implementation of extraction workflows.

Resident Engine Maintenance

Ongoing tuning and updates to the hospital-resident extraction engine.

Strategic Audit

Periodic performance monitoring and quality reports to maintain scientific integrity.
