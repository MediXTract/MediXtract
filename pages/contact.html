Our Mission

"Transforming unstructured clinical data into the foundation of tomorrow's medical research."

MediXtract was founded on the conviction that healthcare institutions can unlock vast amounts of underutilised clinical knowledge through technology developed in genuine partnership with clinicians. We are dedicated to making healthcare data actionable for science, operating at the boundary where technology and medicine meet.

Origins

A simple question that sparked a journey.

MediXtract began with a frustration shared by clinicians across European academic medical centres: enormous amounts of valuable clinical data locked inside unstructured documents, inaccessible to research without years of manual abstraction work.

We started with a simple question: what if building a research-ready registry could be automated — without sacrificing the clinical precision that makes registry data valuable? Our first answer came through the NEC registry project at Inselspital Bern.
